LIBRARY

A library is a collection of materials, books or media that are easily accessible for use and not just for display purposes.It is responsible for housing updated information in order to meet the user's needs on a daily basis.A library, which may vary widely in size, may be organized for use and maintained by a public body such as a government, an institution/ schools, a corporation, or a private individual. In addition to providing materials, libraries also provide the services of librarians who are trained and experts at finding, selecting, circulating and organizing information and at interpreting information needs, navigating and analyzing very large amounts of information with a variety of resources.
